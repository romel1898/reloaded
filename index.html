Popular Cocktails

  • Old Fashioned

  • The Bourbon Old Fashioned is one of the most classic whiskey cocktails of all time. It's just three ingredients, but bourbon lovers never tire of it.

  • Negroni

  • A Negroni is an Italian cocktail, made of one part gin, one part vermouth rosso, and one part Campari, garnished with orange peel. It is considered an aperitivo.

  • Daiquiri

  • Daiquiri is a family of cocktails whose main ingredients are rum, citrus juice, and sugar or other sweetener.

  • Dry Martini

  • The martini is a cocktail made with gin and vermouth, and garnished with an olive or a lemon twist.

  • Margarita

  • Margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice often served with salt on the rim of the glass.

  • Espresso Martini

  • The espresso martini is a cold, coffee-flavored cocktail made with vodka, espresso coffee, and coffee liqueur.
